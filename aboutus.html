ABOUT US

Our lawyers are renowned for their professional in-depth analytical and problem solving skills in a comprehensive range of legal disciplines and total devotion to meeting clients demands at short notice.

We have represented local and international clients at the specialized tribunals in Kenya including: the Sports Tribunal, the Industrial Property Tribunal, the National Environmental Tribunal, the Public Private Partnerships Tribunal, the Public Procurement and Administrative Review Board, the Political Parties Tribunal and before the Registrar of Trademarks. We also provide a day to day basis rate professional services at the Magistrates courts, High Court, Court of Appeal and the Supreme Court both at the Capital city Nairobi and around the country.

Our clients include local and international corporations and ordinary citizens in their ordinary course of personal and commercial endeavors. We work with them practically through consultations, assessment, investigations, utmost con dentiality and strategic planning with a view of achieving commercial solutions.
